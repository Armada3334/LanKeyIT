LanKeyIT, LLC Special Services

Special assistance for small businesses just getting started.

In efforts to assist Entrepreneurs in the Fort Wayne and surrounding area, LankeyIT, LLC is able to provide  discounted services and consulting. If you are a business that is just getting started, contact us for help with technology implementation. This is a great way for us to make sure home-grown small bussinesses thrive!

Here is why,

I grew up near Fort Wayne and moved to the city in 2006. I have worked for and with many small businesses while building my knowledge and experience in the I.T. industry. Over the years, I have observed the gradual revitalization of the city. I realize that the success of local small business greatly impact the success of the city. I want to see the local small business community flourish.

When I started the process of setting up my business I quickly realized that I needed help. Fortunately, I knew working professionals that were willing to give me assistance. I believe many people give up on starting their small business due to frustration and the complexity of the process. These are businesses that could have provided jobs and helped to improve the small communities in the city of Fort Wayne. At LanKeyIT, LLC we want to ensure that people have equality of opportunity when starting a business.

The RMM Agent
