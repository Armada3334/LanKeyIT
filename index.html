Welcome to LanKeyIT, LLC

Learn about some of the most common services we provide.

  • BREAK/FIX ISSUES: We are available for any immediate Break/Fix issues you have. We work with servers, workstations, Voip phones, backup systems, antivirus, firewall/routers, wired and wirelss networking.

  • MANAGED SERVICES CONTRACT: With the use of Labtech remote management software, We can proactively monitor your systems. This allows us to keep your systems running optimally and catch small issues before they become catastrophies. You can maintain business continuity and have inhouse I.T. for a fraction of the price.

  • NETWORKING (WIRED AND WIRELESS): Our technicians can implement high speed wired and wireless networks in any environment. We are knowledgable in popular technologies like Cat5e, Cat6, and Fiber Optics. Also, we can harden wireless networks for security.

  • DISASTER/RECOVERY SYSTEMS: In the case of a software crash and/or hardware failure of your systems, a backup/recovery plan needs to be implemented that allows for quick recovery of necessary data and even an entire computer systems.

  • ANTI-VIRUS SOFTWARE: Every day there are new exploits discovered in commonly used software. Some of these viruses can render all data on the network unreadable by encrypting it. This threat is known as ransomware. Every network should be monitored with anti-virus software that is effective at fighting these threats.
